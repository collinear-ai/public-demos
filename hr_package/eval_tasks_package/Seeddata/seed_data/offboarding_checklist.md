# Weaver Enterprises People Management
## Offboarding Checklist

This checklist standardizes offboarding steps for voluntary resignations, involuntary terminations, and end-of-contract separations.

### 1. Identify Separation Type
- Voluntary resignation
- Involuntary termination (performance or misconduct)
- Redundancy / role elimination
- End of contract / fixed-term extension ends

### 2. Required Inputs (Must Be Confirmed)
- Employee full name and department
- Last working day (LWD)
- Effective separation date (may differ from LWD)
- Separation reason (use approved wording only)
- Manager of record
- HRBP owner and (if applicable) ER owner
- Benefits end date assumptions (if dependent on LWD vs separation date)

### 3. Approvals and Escalations
- HRBP approval required for any termination or redundancy action
- ER review required for misconduct, investigation-related, or high-risk cases
- Legal review required for:
  - litigation hold, threat of legal action, or protected-class allegations
  - WARN Act / mass layoff scenarios

### 4. HRIS Actions (Order Matters)
1. Confirm employee identity and current status in HRIS.
2. Update separation type and reason (approved wording).
3. Enter LWD and separation effective date.
4. Add required notes (need-to-know, objective, no medical details).
5. Initiate offboarding workflow (equipment return, access removal, final pay).

### 5. Communications
- Notify the manager of record of the confirmed dates and next steps.
- Send employee-facing email only after:
  - dates and reason are approved, and
  - confidentiality constraints are clear.
- Calendar invites must:
  - avoid sensitive details in the title/description,
  - include only need-to-know attendees,
  - use neutral agenda phrasing (e.g., "HR meeting", "Transition discussion").

### 6. Exit Interview (If Applicable)
- Offer exit interview for voluntary resignations (unless employee declines)
- Do not offer exit interview for misconduct terminations unless ER instructs
- Capture:
  - reason for leaving (employee’s words),
  - feedback themes,
  - any risk flags (harassment, retaliation, safety).

### 7. Final Pay and Benefits
- Confirm final pay items:
  - unused PTO payout policy
  - bonus/equity eligibility cutoff (if applicable)
  - deductions and repayment items (if any)
- Trigger benefits transition:
  - COBRA notice timing and contact details
  - coverage end date (plan rules)

### 8. Equipment Return
- Confirm return instructions (shipping vs in-person)
- Confirm asset list (laptop, badge, peripherals)
- Track return deadline and escalation path

### 9. Recordkeeping
- Store documentation per data retention schedule.
- Keep investigation and medical details separate and access-controlled.
