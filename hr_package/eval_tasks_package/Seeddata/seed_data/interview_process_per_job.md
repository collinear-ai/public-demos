## Feedback & Evaluation

### Feedback Submission

- All interviewers must submit written feedback within **4 hours** of interview completion
- Use the standard feedback template in the ATS
- Include specific examples and evidence for each competency assessed

### Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Strong Hire | Exceptional candidate, exceeded expectations in all areas |
| Hire | Solid candidate, met expectations across assessed competencies |
| Inclined to Hire | Good candidate with minor concerns, would support with reservations |
| Inclined Not to Hire | Concerns outweigh positives, would not recommend |
| No Hire | Did not meet expectations, clear gaps in required competencies |

### Bar Raiser Role

The Bar Raiser serves as an independent evaluator to ensure we maintain a high hiring bar:
- Has veto power on hiring decisions
- Focuses on long-term potential and culture fit
- Ensures fair and consistent evaluation across candidates
