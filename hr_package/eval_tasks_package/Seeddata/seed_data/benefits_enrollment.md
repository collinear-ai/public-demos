## 1. WELCOME TO YOUR BENEFITS

Weaver Enterprises provides a comprehensive benefits package for your health, financial security, and work-life balance. This guide helps you understand your options and complete enrollment.

### 1.1 Important Dates

**New Hire Enrollment:**
- Eligibility begins: First day of the month following 30 days of employment
- Enrollment deadline: Within 30 days of hire date
- Coverage effective date: First day of month following enrollment

**Annual Open Enrollment:**
- Enrollment period: November 1-30 (prior year)
- Coverage effective date: January 1
- Changes apply for the full plan year unless you have a qualifying life event

**Qualifying Life Events (QLE):**
- You have 31 days from the QLE date to make changes
- Examples: marriage, divorce, birth/adoption, loss of other coverage, change in employment status

### 1.2 Who Is Eligible

**Employees:**
- Full-time employees working 30+ hours per week
- Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week (limited benefits)

**Dependents:**
- Legal spouse or domestic partner
- Children up to age 26
- Disabled dependents over age 26 (with documentation)

### 1.3 How to Enroll

**Online Enrollment:**
1. Log into the benefits portal (link in email)
2. Use your employee ID and temporary password
3. Review benefit options and costs
4. Make your elections and submit by the deadline

## 2. MEDICAL INSURANCE

Weaver Enterprises offers medical coverage through multiple carriers including Premera Blue Cross, UnitedHealthcare, and Aetna. Plan options include PPO and HDHP.

### 2.1 Plan Tiers

- **Employee Only** — Individual coverage
- **Employee + 1** — Employee and one dependent
- **Family** — Employee and all eligible dependents

### 2.2 Coverage and Cost Sharing

Details for each plan (deductibles, copays, out-of-pocket limits) are in the plan comparison tool on the benefits portal. Contact the Benefits team (Rachel Green, James Liu) or use #Benefits-Questions for plan-specific questions.

## 3. OTHER BENEFITS

- **Dental & Vision** — Available during open enrollment or with a QLE
- **401(k)** — Company match; enroll or update via the benefits portal
- **Leave** — PTO, parental leave, and other leave types per Weaver Enterprises policy
