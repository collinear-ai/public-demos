# Weaver Enterprises People Management
## Accommodation Request SOP

This SOP covers workplace accommodation requests (including accessibility needs, schedule flexibility, and return-to-work adjustments).

### Scope
- Applies to employees and contractors.
- Covers disability accommodations, temporary medical accommodations, and ergonomic needs.
- Does not cover immigration/legal accommodations.

### Intake Channels
- Employee requests via HR chat/email
- Manager requests on behalf of employee (with employee consent)
- HRBP/ER referrals

### Required Intake Information
- Employee name, department, manager
- Requested accommodation (what is needed)
- Duration (temporary vs ongoing)
- Work impact (what tasks/meetings are affected)
- Documentation status:
  - if medical documentation is required, request only what is necessary
  - store documentation separately from general HR notes

### Triage and Ownership
- Default owner: HRBP for the employee’s department
- Escalate to ER if:
  - there is an active conflict, disciplinary action, or investigation
  - the request is tied to a complaint (harassment/retaliation)
- Escalate to Legal if:
  - litigation threat, formal legal complaint, or high-risk precedent

### Decision and Approval
- HRBP reviews feasibility with manager
- If cost/equipment is required, coordinate with IT/Facilities per equipment process
- Approval outcomes:
  - Approved as requested
  - Approved with modification (document rationale)
  - Denied (document objective reason; offer alternatives where possible)

### Implementation Steps
1. Confirm approved accommodation details in writing (email recap).
2. Update HRIS notes with a non-medical summary (need-to-know only).
3. If scheduling changes are needed:
   - update recurring meetings, focus blocks, or on-call coverage planning
4. If equipment is needed:
   - submit equipment request and track fulfillment

### Privacy Rules
- Do not include diagnosis or detailed medical info in calendar invites or broad emails.
- Share only what is necessary for implementation.

### Time Expectations
- Acknowledge receipt: within 1 business day
- First triage/clarification: within 3 business days
- Decision target: within 10 business days (or provide an ETA)
