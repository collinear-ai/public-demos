# Weaver Enterprises People Management
## Compliance Training Calendar

This calendar defines mandatory trainings, deadlines, and escalation rules.

### Annual Mandatory Trainings (All Employees)
| Course Code | Course Name | Frequency | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| COC-101 | Code of Conduct | Annual | Jan 31 |
| HAR-201 | Anti-Harassment & Discrimination | Annual | Jan 31 |
| SEC-110 | Information Security Awareness | Annual | Jan 31 |
| PRIV-120 | Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA) | Annual | Jan 31 |

### Role-Based Mandatory Trainings
| Role | Course Code | Course Name | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| People Managers | MGR-301 | Performance Management Essentials | Within 30 days of becoming a manager |
| HR / People Team | ER-401 | Investigation Fundamentals | Within 60 days of joining People team |
| Finance | FIN-210 | Financial Controls & Expense Policy | Mar 31 |

### New Hire Deadlines
- Complete annual mandatory trainings within 7 calendar days of start date.

### Non-Compliance Escalation
1. Day 1 after deadline: automated reminder email.
2. Day 7 after deadline: manager notified.
3. Day 14 after deadline: HRBP notified; employee required to complete within 7 days.
4. Day 21 after deadline: ER review for potential disciplinary action.
