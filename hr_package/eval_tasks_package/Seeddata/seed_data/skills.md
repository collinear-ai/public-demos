# HR People Management — Implicit Task Conventions

These are standard conventions that apply to every people management task.

## HRIS Updates
- Every action that changes candidate status, interview details, or offer terms must be logged in HRIS
- Update the relevant HRIS fields needed for an accurate record

## Calendar Events
- Include timezone in all calendar events
- Include all relevant participants on the invite

## Stakeholder Communication
- When a change affects someone, notify them (e.g., swapping an interviewer → notify the old and new interviewer)
- When you complete an action someone requested, confirm it with them
- Internal jargon, acronyms, and process terminology should not appear in candidate emails
- Always check your email inbox at the start of a task — there may be context (candidate details, prior approvals, policy clarifications, or instructions from stakeholders) that affects how the task should be completed.

## Privacy
- Do not include compensation details in calendar invites or group communications
- Medical, accommodation details, and other personal info should only be shared with need-to-know parties

## Communication Channels & Authority

### Chat — DMs and Seeded Channels
- NPCs can receive and respond in 1-1 direct messages and in seeded Rocket.Chat channels.
- If a task requires `#channel` usage, that channel must be present in `seed_group_channels`.
- Agents cannot create channels/groups during execution.

### Authority Deferral
- When opinions or decisions conflict across people in a decision (e.g., manager vs. employee), defer to the higher authority for the given task according to the org chart. Do not average opinions or side with whoever responded first.
