# Candidate FAQ

## Compensation & Equity

### How is base salary determined?
Base salary is set based on role, level, location, and experience. Each level has a defined range (min/mid/max). Most offers start at or near the midpoint.

### How do RSUs work?
RSUs vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff:
- Year 1: 5% (at 12-month anniversary)
- Year 2: 15% (quarterly vesting)
- Years 3-4: 40% each year (quarterly vesting)

RSUs are taxed as ordinary income when they vest.

### Is the sign-on bonus negotiable?
Sign-on bonuses are used to bridge the gap when candidates have unvested equity at their current company. We can discuss your specific situation.

### What about annual raises and bonuses?
- Annual merit increases: typically 3-8% based on performance
- Annual bonus target varies by level, paid in Q1
- Equity refresh grants: awarded annually based on performance

## Benefits

### When do benefits start?
Benefits effective dates vary by program. Some benefits may begin on your first day, while others may have eligibility requirements or waiting periods (see the relevant plan documents).

### What health insurance options are available?
We offer medical coverage options. For the authoritative plan details (deductibles, copays, out-of-pocket max, network rules), see `health_insurance_plan.md`.

### Is the PTO really unlimited?
Yes, we have an unlimited discretionary PTO policy. We encourage at least 2 weeks per year. Manager approval is required but rarely denied for reasonable requests.

### What's the 401(k) match?
See `401K_policy.md` for eligibility, match formula, and vesting details.

## Work Arrangements

### What does hybrid mean?
Our hybrid model requires 3 days per week in the office (typically Tue-Thu). 2 days can be worked remotely. Core collaboration hours are 10am-3pm local time.

### Can I work fully remote?
Fully remote arrangements are considered case-by-case and depend on role requirements. Discuss with your hiring manager if this is important to you.

### What about relocation?
- **Domestic moves**: $10,000 lump sum
- **International moves**: $25,000 lump sum + visa sponsorship
- **Temporary housing**: 30 days covered for domestic moves

## Timeline & Process

### When will I hear back after my interview?
We aim to make decisions within 5 business days of your final interview. Your recruiter will reach out with next steps.

### How long is the offer valid?
Offers typically expire within 3-5 business days. If you need more time, please discuss with your recruiter.

### What happens after I accept?
1. Background check initiated (typically 3-5 business days)
2. I-9 verification and new hire paperwork
3. Laptop and equipment shipped before start date
4. Welcome email with Day 1 logistics

### Can I negotiate my start date?
Yes, start dates are flexible within reason. Most candidates start on a Monday. We typically can accommodate 2-4 weeks from offer acceptance.

## Team & Role

### What's the onboarding like?
- Week 1: Orientation, compliance training, team introductions
- Weeks 2-4: Role-specific training, 1:1s with stakeholders
- First 90 days: Defined onboarding projects with clear success metrics

## Visa & Work Authorization

### Do you sponsor visas?
Yes, we sponsor H-1B, L-1, and green cards for eligible roles. Discuss your specific situation with your recruiter.

### I'm on OPT/STEM OPT—is that okay?
Yes, we can hire candidates on OPT or STEM OPT and sponsor H-1B before the authorization expires.
