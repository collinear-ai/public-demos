# Weaver Enterprises People Management
## HRBP Escalation Matrix

Use this matrix to decide who owns a case and when to escalate.

### Ownership Rules (Default)
- HRBP owns: routine people-management support, performance management guidance, promotions, compensation cycles, leave coordination, onboarding/offboarding.
- Employee Relations (ER) owns: investigations, misconduct, harassment/discrimination claims, retaliation claims, high-risk terminations, grievances.
- Legal owns: litigation holds, formal legal complaints, subpoenas, WARN/mass layoff compliance, contract disputes with material risk.
- Benefits & Payroll owns: benefits eligibility/enrollment issues, COBRA, payroll corrections, deductions, wage garnishments.

### Escalation Triggers
- Escalate to ER if:
  - any allegation of harassment/discrimination/retaliation
  - any investigation is requested or required
  - any termination involves misconduct or policy violations
  - a grievance is filed or threatened
- Escalate to Legal if:
  - employee mentions attorney/lawsuit/regulator
  - WARN Act thresholds may be implicated
  - cross-border employment law is involved
  - a severance agreement is requested or negotiated

### Approval Authority (Simplified)
- Routine HRIS updates: HRBP
- Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) initiation: Manager + HRBP
- Termination (performance): Manager + HRBP, ER consulted if sensitive
- Termination (misconduct): ER required, Legal if high risk
- Compensation exceptions (above band midpoint/max): HRBP + Comp/Finance per comp policy
- Benefits exceptions: Benefits & Payroll Director
