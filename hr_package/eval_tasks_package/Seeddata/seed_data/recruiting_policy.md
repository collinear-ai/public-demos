# Weaver Enterprises Recruiting Policy

This document outlines the recruiting and hiring policies for Weaver Enterprises.

## Interview Process

### Interview Stages

1. **Application Review** - Initial screening of applications
2. **Phone Screen** - 30-minute phone interview with recruiter
3. **Technical Interview** - 60-minute technical assessment
4. **Behavioral Interview** - 45-minute behavioral assessment
5. **Panel Interview** - 90-minute interview with multiple interviewers
6. **Final Round** - 120-minute final assessment (may include multiple sessions)

### Interview Scheduling

- Interviews should be scheduled within 2 business days of previous stage completion
- Interview rooms must be reserved at least 24 hours in advance
- Video interviews are available for remote candidates
- Panel interviews require Conference Room A or Panel Interview Room

### Interview Rooms

- **Conference Room A** - Main interview room, capacity 6
- **Conference Room B** - Secondary interview room, capacity 4
- **Video Conference Room** - Equipped for remote interviews
- **Panel Interview Room** - Large room for panel interviews, capacity 10

## Candidate Pipeline Management

### Pipeline Stages

- Application Review
- Phone Screen Scheduled
- Phone Screen Completed
- Technical Interview Scheduled
- Technical Interview Completed
- Panel Interview Scheduled
- Panel Interview Completed
- Offer Extended
- Offer Accepted
- Offer Declined
- Rejected

### Candidate Communication

- All candidate communications should be sent via email
- Response time should be within 24 hours for candidate inquiries
- Interview confirmations should be sent at least 48 hours in advance
- Rejection notifications should be sent promptly after decision

## Job Requisition Process

1. Hiring manager creates job requisition
2. Requisition reviewed and approved by VP of Talent Acquisition
3. Job posting published
4. Candidates apply
5. Recruiting team screens and schedules interviews
6. Hiring decision made
7. Offer extended
8. Onboarding process begins
