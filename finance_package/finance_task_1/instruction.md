# KO Investment Manager Analysis

## Objective

Build an Investment Manager ready spreadsheet for The Coca-Cola Company (KO) that answers three core questions about the business using FY2022–FY2024 SEC filings (Coca-Cola 10-Ks for fiscal years ended 2024-12-31, 2023-12-31, 2022-12-31) and market data as-of the 2024-12-31 close.

## Deliverable

One spreadsheet with three tabs. You determine the appropriate line items, metrics, and presentation — but every input must have a precise filing reference (form type, fiscal year end, filing date, statement or note name, and page/section identifier visible in the filing).

## Tab 1: "Revenue Quality"

**Goal:** Help an Investment Manager understand how Coca-Cola's top-line growth is performing.

Address the following:
- How much of revenue growth over the period was driven by volume versus pricing/mix?
- How diversified is the revenue base geographically, and which regions are contributing the most to growth?
- What is the mix between capital-light concentrate/syrup operations versus capital-intensive finished product/bottling operations, and how has this evolved?

Pull whatever SEC filing inputs you need to support this analysis. Surface the metrics that best answer these questions for a beverage company.

## Tab 2: "Cash Economics"

**Goal:** Show whether Coca-Cola converts its reported earnings into actual cash efficiently, and how it allocates that cash.

Address the following:
- What is the relationship between reported profitability and free cash flow generation? Is conversion consistent or volatile?
- How capital-intensive is the business, and is intensity increasing or decreasing?
- What portion of cash generation is returned to shareholders versus reinvested in the business?
- How efficient is the company's working capital management, e.g., cash conversion cycle?

Select the inputs and construct the metrics that best illustrate the cash economics of a global beverage concentrate business.

## Tab 3: "Valuation Context"

**Goal:** Give the Investment Manager a quick snapshot of the price earnings ratio and how the stock has performed.

As-of the 2024-12-31 close:
- Compute a market cap and enterprise value (clearly define your net debt/cash methodology and justify it given Coca-Cola's balance sheet structure).
- Present the valuation multiples most relevant for a staples/beverage comps analysis — you decide which 3–4 multiples are most informative and why.
- Show KO price returns (not total return) versus KO's key competitor and the S&P500 over a 1-year window (2023-12-31 to 2024-12-31) and a 3-year window (2021-12-31 to 2024-12-31).

## Documentation Standards

- No hardcoded figures without a visible source citation.
- Reconcile sign conventions with how Coca-Cola presents its cash flow statement; note any adjustments.
- Where analyst judgment is required (e.g., interpreting MD&A narrative, choosing between disclosure formats that changed across years), document your reasoning briefly in the spreadsheet.
- Provide formatting which matches the template doc file in the reference folder and name the final file in the workspace folder as a google sheet with name 'output_file.xlsx'.
